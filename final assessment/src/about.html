Site and Creator

Hi, I'm Gyeongrok Oh, and seneca student from 19 years old, the creator of this Tic-Tac-Toe Game website! As an avid game enthusiast and web developer, I decided to create this website to showcase the classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe in a digital format. Tic-Tac-Toe holds a special place in my heart as it was one of the first games I learned to play during my childhood. Its simplicity and strategic elements have always fascinated me, and I wanted to share this nostalgic experience with others through this digital adaptation.

Why Tic-Tac-Toe?

Tic-Tac-Toe, also known as noughts and crosses, is a timeless game that transcends generations. Its easy-to-learn rules make it accessible to players of all ages, while its strategic depth provides a challenge for those looking to sharpen their tactical skills. By creating this website, I aim to celebrate the game's rich history, engage players in a fun and interactive way, and promote the joy of playing Tic-Tac-Toe both as a casual pastime and a competitive activity.
