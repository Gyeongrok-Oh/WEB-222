How to Play

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic game that is easy to learn and fun to play.
It is also known as noughts and crosses or Xs and Os.
The game is played on a 3x3 grid, where two players take turns marking the spaces with X or O.
The goal is to make three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
If the grid is full and no one has made three in a row, the game is a tie.

Here are the basic steps to play Tic-Tac-Toe:

1.Decide who will be X and who will be O. X always goes first.

2.Draw a 3x3 grid on a piece of paper or use an online board.

3.Take turns placing your mark on an empty space on the grid. You can only mark one space per turn.

4.Check if you have made three of your marks in a row. If you have, you win the game and can celebrate. If not, continue to step 5.

5.Check if the grid is full. If it is, the game is a tie and no one wins. If not, go back to step 3 and repeat until someone wins or the game is a tie.
